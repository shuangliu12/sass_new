MONOLOG | A Ruby on Sinatra web application for mood tracking and expressive writing People in need of clinical services often reported a lack of support outside of counseling sessions. Inspired by Dr. James Pennebaker's Writing to Heal (2004), I developed Monolog to offer support for people with mood disorders. Monolog keeps track of users' emotional upheavals.
