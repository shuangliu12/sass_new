MEET AND LUNCH | A Ruby on Rails web application for lunch sharing and networking For professionals new to a city, they may have limited lunch options at work and a lack of quality networking. This is where Meet & Lunch comes in. People can cook extra portions for lunch and post their available meals on Meet & Lunch. Other individuals who are tired of restaurants near their office can order a homemade lunch. Meet & Lunch presents a natural way to meet other professionals. Homemade food brings people together.
