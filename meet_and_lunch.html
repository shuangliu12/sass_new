MEET AND LUNCH A Ruby on Rails web application for lunch sharing and networking
Motivation For professionals new to a city, they may have a lack of quality networking and limited lunch options at work. This is where Meet & Lunch comes in. People who love to cook can cook extra portions for lunch and post their available meals on Meet & Lunch. People who are tired of restaurants near their offices can order a homemade lunch. Meet & Lunch presents a natural way to meet other professionals. Homemade food brings people together.
Target Users
Food-seekers: People who don't feel like cooking, but love to try homemade lunches from others. Chefs: People who love to cook and love to share lunches with others.
Go to the App
Landing Page Both Chefs and Food-seekers can sign up or log in. Food-seekers can search for nearby kitchens by location.
Kitchen Page The kitchen page displays an info section with a short description, address and overall rating of the kitchen. The lunch menu displays meals. The review section shows the most recent reviews from Food-seekers. The Chef of this kitchen is able to edit info on the page.
Profile Page The profile page shows a short description of the user and a link to the kitchen page (if the user is a Chef). Since users can be both Chefs and Food-seekers, this page shows the review, order and meal histories of this user. The owner of the page can edit their profile photo and self-introduction on this page.
