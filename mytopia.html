MYTOPIA | A Ruby on Rails web application for neighborhood reviews Apartment hunting is hard, especially for people who have newly moved to a big urban city. Mytopia is the Yelp for rental hunting, where city-dwellers post photos, reviews and ratings of their neighborhoods. Newcomers can use Mytopia for guidance in their housing search. Mytopia also pulls data from Zillow to keep demographic data up-to-date.
