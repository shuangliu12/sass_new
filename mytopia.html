MYTOPIA A Ruby on Rails web application for neighborhood reviews
Motivation Apartment hunting is hard, especially for people who are new to the city. Mytopia is the Yelp for rental hunting, where city-dwellers post photos, reviews and ratings of their neighborhoods. House Hunters can use Mytopia for guidance in their housing search. Mytopia also pulls data from Zillow to keep demographic data up-to-date.
Target Users
House Hunters: People who are new to a city and want to know what a neighborhood is like. Locals: People who are willing to share their experiences about their neigborhood.

Landing Page Both House Hunters and Locals can find a neighborhood by searching by name of the neighborhood, city or state.
Sample Search Results Page If a user conducts a search by city, a list of neighborhoods and their overall ratings will be shown. Users can skip the neigborhoods with unsatisfactory ratings without spending time to research more about these neighorboods.
Neighborhood Page The neighborhood page displays the demographic information of the neighborhood from Zillow. Locals can submit photos of the neighorhood, but the photos won't be shown until admins give approval to avoid spam photos. Locals can give ratings and write reviews of the neighborhoods. Users can also upvote, downvote or comment on any review.
